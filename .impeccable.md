## Design Context

### Users

Households tracking supplement inventory, dosage, run-out timing, restocking, and spend on the go. The primary mobile job is a quick, confident check or update with one hand.

### Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, practical. The interface should reduce uncertainty without adding urgency or noise.

### Aesthetic Direction

Preserve the existing restrained, nature-tinted green system in light and dark themes. On phones, feel like a focused web app: compact chrome, generous touch targets, clear hierarchy, and a persistent bottom navigation affordance where it improves reachability.

### Design Principles

- Preserve the existing desktop information architecture and visual tokens.
- Reflow to a single column on narrow portrait screens instead of shrinking dense layouts.
- Optimize controls for touch with at least 44px hit areas and visible focus/pressed states.
- Keep primary actions and current context reachable with one hand.
- Respect safe areas, reduced motion, and landscape/mobile edge cases.
